Travel Media Brief is built for readers who want to understand travel media as an industry, not just as a stream of posts, videos, and platform updates.

The publication covers how travel stories are discovered, funded, distributed, and trusted. That includes search and social platforms, tourism boards, newsletters, independent publishers, creator partnerships, audience behaviour, and the economics behind travel content.

The goal is practical clarity. Each issue looks at what changed, why it matters, and how it may affect people building, funding, or following travel media.
